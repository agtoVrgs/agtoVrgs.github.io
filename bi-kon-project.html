BI-KON Project

The BI-KON project involved standardizing engineering documents to ensure easy accessibility and comprehension across various departments within the manufacturing plant. This standardization was crucial for efficient reference to product engineering details, specifically for the manufacturing of plastic valves. The project demanded a high proficiency in SolidWorks and PDM to accomplish the tasks effectively.
