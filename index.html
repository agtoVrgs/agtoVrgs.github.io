Candle Machine Project

I designed and built a machine which can process a 20 kg batch of wax, producing 6 big candles each with dimensions of 10 cm in diameter and 49.5 cm in height. The key features of the machine include:

  • Sturdy structure to withstand the forces generated during candle extraction.
  • A power assembly made with simple parts, offering low complexity and cost.
  • An efficient heat exchanger to solidify the candles.
  • An innovative process that consolidates the wax and candlewick in one step.

These features lead to a decrease in building costs and reduction of manufacturing times.
